Short Bio

Nilesh Suriyarachchi received the honors degree in electronic and telecommunications engineering from University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, in 2017.
He is currently a Ph.D. candidate in electrical and computer engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park, MD, USA, working with John S. Baras, Distinguished University Professor and Chair in Systems Engineering.
His research focuses on using optimization, control and learning techniques to harness the collaborative capabilities of connected autonomous vehicles in order to solve many of the existing problems plaguing modern transportation networks.
